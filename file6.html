KrabiClub

Founder of a creative journaling and art community. Hosted the first workshop with 20+ participants and built the community website.

KrabiClub is a creative community and experiential journaling initiative that transforms everyday moments into meaningful artifacts of memory. At its core, KrabiClub embraces the practice of junk journaling — a mindful and artistic way of capturing life’s little experiences, thoughts, and inspirations on paper, not just digitally.

Unlike traditional digital diaries, KrabiClub invites participants to slow down, engage creatively, and preserve life’s fragments — movie tickets from a spontaneous outing, dried flowers from a college fest, handwritten quotes, printed photos, and other tactile memories become part of a personal time capsule. Each page becomes a testament to identity, emotion, and personal history, encouraging reflection and connection.
