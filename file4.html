Teaching Assistant — Deep Learning Lab

Under Dr. Pandyaraju V | July 2025 – Nov 2025

Served as a Teaching Assistant for a master’s-level Deep Learning Lab while still an undergraduate, supporting a cohort of 60 second-year master’s students. This role involved grading assignments, preparing lecture materials, invigilating examinations, and providing conceptual clarity during lab sessions.

Assisted students in understanding and implementing core deep learning concepts, including neural networks, convolutional neural networks (CNNs), GANs, LSTMs, and regularization techniques. Teaching these topics significantly strengthened my own conceptual foundations and highlighted the depth of preparation required to deliver effective instruction.

Teaching Assistant — Software Engineering

Under Dr. Rama Prabha K. P. | Aug 2024 – Dec 2024

Through close mentorship, student teams demonstrated improved project planning, clearer documentation, and more maintainable codebases, leading to stronger overall project outcomes.
